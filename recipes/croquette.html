Potato Croquettes

Description

Originating from the French cuisine, croquettes consist of what is called a binder which is an outside coating made of potatoes or wheat flour. Croquettes have a filling on the inside which may be cheese, meat, or vegies. They are kind of like Rice Balls, but minus the rice.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  1. Mash the potatoes.
  2. Form a ball of mashed potatoes around a cube of cheese.
  3. Roll the ball into the flour, dip them in the beaten eggs, then roll them in the breadcrumbs.
  4. Deep fry the ball in vegetable oil for ~4 minutes.
  5. Remove and enjoy!
